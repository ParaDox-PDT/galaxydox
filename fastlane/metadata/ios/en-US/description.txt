GalaxyDox is a premium NASA explorer that turns public space data into a clean, immersive mobile experience.

Discover:

- Astronomy Picture of the Day with context-rich reading
- Mars rover photography in a gallery-first layout
- Near-Earth Object tracking with readable mission telemetry
- NASA media archive search for images and historical discoveries

Built for repeat discovery:

- fast, focused browsing across core NASA content
- local bookmarks saved on-device
- polished presentation for both editorial reading and dense data
- no ads and no account setup in the current app experience

GalaxyDox is made for people who want to explore astronomy, NASA missions, space imagery, nebulae, galaxies, rover photography, and asteroid approach data from one elegant surface.
